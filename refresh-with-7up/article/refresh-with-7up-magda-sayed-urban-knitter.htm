A chat with Magda

What inspired you to begin expressing your creativity through knitting?

MAGDA - It was what I did with knitting that inspired me. Taking knitting to this other level where functionality wasn’t the main purpose intrigued me. I loved seeing the unplanned arrangement of form that the knitting or crocheting would take on when I covered an object.

When did you learn to knit? Who taught you?

MAGDA - I originally learned from a simple how-to pamphlet when I was 16 years old, because I wanted to make a scarf for a boy I liked. That didn’t work out so I put the needles down for about 15 years… then I saw this lonely, cold door handle on the door of the shop that I ran and decided I needed to make a sweater for it. The rest is history.

What does "Feels Good To Be You" mean to you?

MAGDA -It means to stay positive, live life fully, see the glass half full…

What is your favorite knitted creation that you have done?

MAGDA - Oh! That is like picking your favorite child! I love them all for different reasons.

Do you have a funny or interesting story to share about any of your creations?

MAGDA - This one may take me a while. I can only think of the scary stories!
